Hal Jordan

Hal Jordan, played by Ryan Reynolds, was the son of an air force pilot, until his father died in an airplane accident. Hal inherits the green power ring from a previous Green Lantern who crashed on Earth, and inherited the Green Lantern power. The key to becoming a powerful Green Latntern is to "show no fear", a trait that even as a kid Hal insisted on portraying.
